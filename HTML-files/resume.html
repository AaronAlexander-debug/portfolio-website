Numonix • completed April 2021

This project was the result of a weekend-long experience with the HackVH Virtual High School Hackathon, my first major foray into the field of web/software development. I quickly learned the basics of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS thanks to my collaboration with an awesome team of guys that helped build the Numonix website, tackling a chronic academic need to improve performance with the usage of a customized study plan based on the concept of spaced repetition.

We would, awesomely enough, get into the top 10 of contestants, excluding the beginners! I was personally responsible for the design aspect, creating the logo for the website and creating the YouTube video that helped sell our concept to the judges, which can be viewed here!
